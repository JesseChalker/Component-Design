Writing

Academic Writing

Academic writing is quite formal in the sense that it avoids using informal or casual language. The three main types of language used in academic writing are:

  • Formal Language
  • Objective Language
  • Technical Language

Types of Academic Writing

There are four main types of academic writing that all have their own purpose in academic text. Most academic texts require more than one type to be used:

  • Descriptive
  • Analytical
  • Persuasive
  • Critical

Planning Your Writing

In academic writing, organising and analysing information can be approached in two key ways:

  • Planning Approach
  • Drafting Approach

Structuring Written Work

In some cases, you may need to come up with a structure for a piece of academic text if it hasn't been given already. Two main texts that may need structuring are:

  • Essays
  • Reports
